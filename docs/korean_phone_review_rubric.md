# Korean Phone Review Rubric

## Purpose

This blind package is evidence for seed stability only. It is not a promotion candidate and cannot override the machine-inconclusive lexical result.

For each item, compare randomized A and B, then inspect protected C. Do not use Whisper transcripts or file seed names while scoring.

Score and note:

- intended phoneme identity
- omissions
- insertions or repetitions
- consonant clarity, including tense and aspirated distinctions
- vowel correctness and stability
- phoneme-to-note timing
- pitch stability and transition behavior
- voicing in consonants and silence
- metallic noise, tearing, discontinuity, or clipping
- overall naturalness

Record uncertainty explicitly. A listener preference does not establish a machine lexical pass.
